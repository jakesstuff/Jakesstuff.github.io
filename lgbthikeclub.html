Queer Hiking Group

We go out in nature, and we hike! A place to make new queer connections.

Schedule

This group has three hikes planned each month. One of the hikes will always be short, local, and public, and this is the hike you should first attend if you want to join the group.

Code of Conduct

This group celebrates human diversity in all it's forms. If you identify as queer or LGBT+, you are welcome in this group.*

*If you're transphobic, you are not welcome in this group. If you support the republican party that actively works to attack our community, you are not welcome. If you are a police officer, an occupation that exists to oppress human freedom, you are not welcome.

To protect our members, most event info is not publicly available. If you are interested in joining, please fill out the linked Google Form and attend one of the monthly intro-hikes. Members will receive a link to the Discord Group where all event details are contained. This group is moderated, and comfort and safety is taken seriously.
