Devil's Tower Roadtrip

Duration: 4 Days (The last night runs late. If you are uncomfortable driving after midnight, it is a 5 day trip. You can also choose not to attend the race in Gillette to get home earlier.)

Estimated Cost: $232-$962

Starting/ending in Cheyenne, WY, this roadtrip visits native American sites at the Pine Ridge Reservation in South Dakota. After, it goes to two different national cave sites before Mount Rushmore. After a scenic drive in the Mount Rushmore area, it continues North to Devil's Tower with some side stops on the way. After Devil's tower, it stops for the majority of a day in Gillette, WY to see some museums and an event at the racetrack. There are many hiking opportunities that can be adjusted as desired. The longest is a very easy bike friendly trail that is 7 or 15 miles out and back to see 2 or 3 old mining tunnls. Other opportunities include many 0.5-5 mile hikes at the various parks.

As much as I enjoy rock climbing, you should not climb Devil's Tower. The park voluntarily closes climbing in June, but it is considered by the local native peoples disrespectful to climb the mountain. It is a cool natural formation, but it also a sacred site.

This trip is planned to leave on a Wednesday. This is highly recommended in order to enjoy all of the planned visits. The best time to do this trip is from May-August, otherwise, certain roads and events will no longer be accessible due to seasonal closures.
