Devils Tower Roadtrip

Duration: 4 Days (5 if you are uncomfortable driving after midnight)

Estimated Cost: $192-$962

From Cheyenne, this roadtrip visits native American sites at the Pine Ridge Reservation in South Dakota. After, it goes to two different national cave sites before Mount Rushmore. After a scenic drive in the Mount Rushmore area, it continues North to Devil's Tower with some side stops on the way. After Devil's tower, it stops for the majority of a day in Gillette, WY to see some museums and an event at the racetrack.

This trip is planned to leave on a Wednesday. This is highly recommended in order to enjoy all of the planned visits. The best time to do this trip is from May-August, otherwise, certain roads and events will no longer be accessible due to seasonal closures.
